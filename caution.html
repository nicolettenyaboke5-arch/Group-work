About Us

Welcome to Aethelgard University, New Zealand's premier institution of higher learning, where tradition, excellence, and innovation come together. Nestled in the breathtaking city of Queenstown, surrounded by majestic mountains and crystal clear lakes, Aethelgard offers students an inspiring environment in which to learn, grow, and succeed. Renowned for its royal heritage and prestigious reputation, the university is home to some of the world's finest lecturers, researchers, and industry professionals who are dedicated to providing an exceptional educational experience. At Aethelgard University, students receive more than a world class education they become part of a vibrant and supportive community. Our academic programmes are designed to challenge and inspire, while our extensive co-curricular opportunities, including leadership programmes, sports, cultural societies, innovation clubs, and community service initiatives, ensure that every student develops valuable life skills beyond the classroom. We believe that success comes from balancing academic achievement with personal growth. To make quality education accessible to talented students worldwide, Aethelgard University proudly offers a range of merit based and need-based scholarships for outstanding local and international applicants. With state of the art facilities, a welcoming campus, diverse student community, and unwavering commitment to excellence, Aethelgard University is more than a place to study it's a place to discover your potential, build lifelong friendships, and prepare for a future of leadership and success.

Explore Campus Clubs

Chess Club

Chess Club

Smart moves and good company. We hang out, play casual games, and help each other get better. From your first pawn push to your final checkmate, everyone is welcome.

Meeting Day: 3:40 PM

Membership Fee: $300

Why join? Chess accelerates your skill development, connects you with a passionate community, and gives you access to competitive in-person events and mentorship.

Anime Club

A gathering place for fans of Japanese animation, manga, and culture to watch, discuss, and share creative ideas.

Meeting Day: Fridays at 5:00 PM

Membership Fee: $5/semester (covers snacks)

Why Join? Connect with fellow fans, participate in cosplay workshops, and enjoy weekly screening nights with free popcorn.

Coding Club

A collaborative hub for building software projects, learning new programming languages, and preparing for technical careers.

Meeting Day: Wednesdays at 4:30 PM

Membership Fee: Free

Why Join? Gain real-world team development experience, participate in hackathons, and build stellar portfolio projects.

Photography Club

Dedicated to exploring the art of visual storytelling through composition, lighting, and digital editing.

Meeting Day: Saturdays at 10:00 AM (Field Trips)

Membership Fee: $15/semester (covers gear rental)

Why Join? Master your camera settings, explore scenic locations during group photowalks, and showcase your work in our termly gallery exhibition.

Debate Club

Formulate structured arguments, sharpen your public speaking, and dive into respectful, structured discussions on major global issues.

Meeting Day: Tuesdays at 5:30 PM

Membership Fee: Free

Why Join? Overcome public speaking anxiety, build unmatched critical research skills, and travel to intercollegiate tournaments.

Robotics Club

Where hardware meets software. We design, wire, and program custom autonomous machines to solve unique engineering challenges.

Meeting Day: Thursdays at 4:00 PM

Membership Fee: $20/year (covers microcontrollers and sensors)

Why Join? Get hands-on experience with 3D printing, circuit design, and Arduino programming while building robots that actually move.

Environmental Club

A passionate, action-oriented group focused on leading local conservation, community gardening, and campus recycling initiatives.

Meeting Day: Thursdays at 3:00 PM

Membership Fee: Free

Why Join? Make a measurable, positive impact on local ecosystems, learn about sustainable living, and get your hands dirty in the campus garden.

Music Club

A space for vocalists, instrumentalists, and producers to jam, write music together, and organize acoustic live sessions.

Meeting Day: Wednesdays at 7:00 PM

Membership Fee: Free

Why Join? Find bandmates to collaborate with, gain stage performance confidence, and access our shared acoustic practice room.
